Description

This is a classic Filipino dish called chicken adobo. It is my personal favorite Filipino dish and has been since I was around 15 or 16. Around that age, I learned to appreciate the sweet undertones of brown sugar under the dish's strong foreground provided by bay leaves and large amounts of garlic.

The dish can be made with other protein sources such as pork or beef, but the use of chicken thighs provides the stew's broth with a thick consistency as a result of dissolved amounts of chicken skin. Consequently, my favorite substitute for chicken thighs is pork belly.
