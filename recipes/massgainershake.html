I will provide you with a drink that looks and tastes like an In-n-Out strawberry shake, which will make your arms 57 inches in circumference and your legs ripping through the jeans you've been wearing since high school.

This protein shake has about 41 grams of protein in it. That is a lot so do make sure that you are drinking a high amount of water every day. I would not consider this shake as a meal replacement, but rather as a supplement to your consistent diet.

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen strawberries
  • 31 grams vanilla Optimum Nutrition Gold Standard Whey Protein Powder
  • 55 grams dry oatmeal
  • 170 grams Fage Nonfat Plain Greek Yogurt
  • An accessible source of drinking water
  • A blender (preferably the Nutribullet PRO 900W Nutrient Extractor Blender if you want to be like Isaiah Glenn Cruz himself)

Directions

  1. First, place your strawberries at the bottom of the blender.
  2. Next, add your oatmeal to the blender.
  3. Next, add your whey protein. Slowly add drinking water to this mixture, letting the water fill in the gaps between these ingredients until the mixture begins to rise.
  4. Add the yogurt. Add water until the peak of the yogurt is just barely poking out (roughly 1 centimeter).
  5. Close the blender lid and shake the container. Blend for about 10 seconds, shake the blending apparatus, then blend for another 10 seconds.
  6. Drink the shake. Once you're done, add drinking water to the shake bottle or blender, shake it until all the bits are hit by the water, and drink the dregs of the shake. You gotta GET IN ALL THAT NUTRITION!!!
  7. Do your dishes to maintain your discipline.
